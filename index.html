Our Mission

Provide, prepare, produce.

Our mission is to develop the future scientists and engineers involved in quantum computing through community, education, and hands-on research and projects.

Provide

Community

Our goal is to provide a community of people who are interested in quantum computing. We cultivate a community by providing casual social events, such as mixers.

Prepare

Education

We educate the community by hosting workshops, visits to local companies, discussions with professors, and teaching opportunities.

Produce

Research

We want our community to solve problems in the field of quantum computing. We provide a space for this through paper reading groups, hackathons and other projects.
